Breakdancing History

B-boying is a form of hip hop dancing which is popularly known as breakdancing. It consists of top or up rock, footwork, spinning moves (power moves), and freezes. B-boying came from Bronx, NY. The term "B-boy" or "B-boying" was created by Kool Herc who was a DJ spinning at block parties in the Bronx back in the days. B-Boys/B-Girls means break boys/girls and they were called so because they dance to the break part of music.
